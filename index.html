Our Story

25 Years of
Engineering Trust

Kotibhaskar Industries was founded in 2000 with a singular focus: building material handling equipment that genuinely works in Indian industrial conditions. From a factory floor in Satara's MIDC, we have grown into one of Maharashtra's most trusted manufacturers.

Our team has developed over 80 innovative products across material handling equipment, trolleys, and wheels. We recently added a state-of-the-art manufacturing line for wheels and castors. Growing at 30–35% annually, we are proud to count leading OEMs, large-scale industries, and SPM manufacturers among our customers.

Every product leaves our facility having been critically tested against our quality parameters — because we know that equipment failure on a factory floor isn't just inconvenient, it's costly.
