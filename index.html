Our Story

25 Years of
Engineering Trust

Kotibhaskar Industries has been serving Indian industries for over 20 years with reliable material handling equipment, industrial wheels, and caster solutions. Built on quality, durability, and customer trust, the company supplies products to manufacturers, warehouses, engineering firms, and industrial facilities across India.

Our mission is to deliver robust and dependable mobility solutions that improve efficiency, safety, and productivity in industrial environments.

From standard caster wheels to customized material handling equipment, we continue to innovate and grow while maintaining the values that built our reputation.
